## 👤 About Me

I'm a **Data Scientist and ML Engineer** focused on building end-to-end solutions — from raw data pipelines to deployed models. My work spans supervised and unsupervised learning, natural language processing, and analytics, with a consistent emphasis on systems that deliver real business value.

I care about clean experimentation, reproducible workflows, and translating complex outputs into decisions that non-technical stakeholders can act on.
